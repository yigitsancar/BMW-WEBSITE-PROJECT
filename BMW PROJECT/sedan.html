The BMW 3 Series Sedan M cars combine a striking four-door, three-compartment design, powerful proportions, and sporty features unique to the M family. Leading the pack with an impressive 510 hp engine power and 650 Nm torque is the BMW M3 Competition Sedan. Equipped with a high-performance BMW M TwinPower Turbo engine, an 8-speed M Steptronic Sport transmission with Drivelogic, optional M xDrive all-wheel drive, Active M Differential, and technologies derived from motorsports, the car promises maximum driving dynamics both in daily use and on race tracks. The sporty BMW M3 Sedan generates 480 hp engine power and 550 Nm torque. The combination of rear-wheel drive system and the ideal use of the 6-speed manual M transmission offers an extremely dynamic driving style, turning the car into a purebred racer. The model range is complemented by the BMW M340i xDrive, BMW M340i, and BMW M340d xDrive Sedan models. These powerful sports sedans inspire with their attractive designs as well as their balanced combination of sporty features, efficiency, and suitability for daily use.

BMW M3 Competition Sedan:
- Fuel consumption (liters/100 km) (average): 9.8
- CO2 emissions (g/km) (average): 224–223

The BMW 3 Series Sedan not only delivers exceptional driving dynamics with its new sporty design but also offers comprehensive and modern technology support for the driver.
- Back-Up Assistant and other intelligent driving assistant systems ensure ideal driving comfort in all conditions.
- An innovative BMW Curved Display with a driver-focused cockpit and touchscreen operation concept.

BMW 320i Sedan:
- Fuel consumption (liters/100 km) (average): 7.3-8.2
- CO2 emissions (g/km) (average): 164-184

The BMW M5 CS, BMW M5 Competition, BMW M5, and BMW M550i xDrive Sedan offer exceptional performance in their respective segments.

- The BMW M5 CS represents the most powerful BMW M5 Sedan model ever produced, with an extraordinary 635 horsepower capable of accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds. This limited-production special model, with its exclusive M suspension and design components, guarantees the pure feeling of motorsport.

- The BMW M5 Competition, with its maximum power of 625 horsepower and performance-optimized suspension settings with M xDrive, surpasses even the highest expectations. Its high-performance sports sedan design, offered in glossy black, visually accentuates its potential.

- The BMW M5, equipped with M xDrive hardware designed for maximum traction and driving dynamics, also features a suspension that meets all requirements for sporty driving comfort on long distances. Combined with the 600 horsepower 8-cylinder M engine, this suspension highlights driving dynamics characterized by power and agility.

- Completing this quartet is the BMW M550i xDrive Sedan, featuring a 530 horsepower engine. The BMW 5 Series Sedan strikes an impressive balance between sporty driving, comfort, and efficiency, leaving a remarkable impression.
